Frequently Asked Questions

How can I Join AjoDAO Today?

Simply head over to AjoDAOs overview page, there you will find the "Join DAO" button. Clicking on the button will pop up a proposal titled "Propose to Add Member to Group". Fill out the required information and send in your proposal to join AjoDAO.

What makes AjoDAO Different form other Credit Unions?

The AjoDAO have found a practical use-case for blockchain technology. We rely on the transparency of a public ledger that is the blockchain, so anyone can see how funds are being moved around within the DAO ecosystem.

How does AjoDAO differ from other online contribution clubs?

Our growing number of contributors brings people from around the globe as a community. We all must decide by votes how the organization operates i.e. member decided the contributions amount, payment dates, etc...

What if a member decides to opt-out of contributions?

AjoDAO members are backed by a piggy bank mechanism, to help fund the next payment cycle. AjoDAO also restricts members from leaving the group until the remaining members have been paid in full.
